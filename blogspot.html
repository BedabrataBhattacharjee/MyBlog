All About the iPhone 13 Series

Author: Bedabrata Bhattacharjee

iphone 13, introduced on the 24th of September is the Apple's newest flagship phones. The successor of the iPhone 12 Mini, iPhone 13 Mini comes with a 5.4 inch display, while the iPhone 13 with 6.1 inch display is the replacement for iPhone 12. Both these new iPhones are pretty identical. The iPhone 13 series features flat edges, an aluminium enclosure, a glass back. It has also became a bit thick (7.65mm). It comes in various colours including Pink, Blue, Midnight etc.

Both these models feature Super Retina XDR Display that are 28% bright. The iPhone 13 Mini has a 2340*1080 resolution with 476 pixels per inch, whereas the iPhone 13 has a 2532*1170 resolution with 460 pixels per inch.

The all new iPhone 13 series' camera system has been updated and the notch is now smaller. It offers a Ceramic Shield for better protection against water and dust, it can tolerate upto 6 metres of water for upto 30 minutes.

Although everyone was hoping that it would come with a m1 chip but it came with an upgraded A15 Bionic Chip (6- core CPU) and a 4-core GPU.

There is a diagonal dual-lens rear camera with 12 megapixels Wide and Ultra-Wide camera. The Wide camera comes with an much improved f1.6 aperture that lets in 47% more light and Sensor-Shift Stabilization, while the Ultra-Wide one comes features an improved f/2.4 aperture for better photography at night. It supports 4K vide recording upto 60 FPS. Along with Potrait, Time-Lapse and other photographic modes, the iPhone 13 series enables us to seamlessly shift focus from one subject to another. The front facing camera features 12 megapixels

5G connectivity is included. WiFi 6 and Bluetooth 5.0 are also there. The new iPhone 13 series offers dual eSIM support and nano-SIM slot.

Battery life has improved significantly. The size of battery has also increased. It offers 19 hours of video playback. he iPhone 13 mini offers up to 1.5 hours more battery life than the iPhone 12 mini, and the iPhone 13 offers up to 2.5 hours more battery life than the iPhone 12. Storage space starts at 128 GB and goes till 512 GB.

There is no power adapter or EarPods included but a Type-C to Lihtning cable included.
